How to Use Our Website

  1. Authentication: Sign up for a new account or log in to access personalized features.
  2. All Routes Display: View all available train routes to plan your journey.
  3. Search for a Specific Route: Easily search for train routes by entering your departure and destination stations along with the travel date.
  4. Ticket Information: Access details of your booked tickets, including seat assignments and journey details.
  5. Profile Editor: Update your profile information to keep your account details current.
  6. Station Information: Find detailed information about each railway station, including location and amenities.
  7. Train Information: Learn about the different trains in service, including their features and capacities.
